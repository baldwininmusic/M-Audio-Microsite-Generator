Introducing the M-Audio M3-8 Studio Monitor

The state-of-the-art M3-8 studio monitor features an advanced and sophisticated design that gives you the clarity, power, and coverage to step your mixes up to the next level. Utilizing three individual class AB amplifiers, high quality low-, mid-, and hi-frequency drivers, and a precision-designed cabinet and baffle, the M3-8 delivers truly spectacular three-way sound with greater midrange detail, higher power-handling and broader sound coverage—from the same compact cabinet size as a standard two-way monitor.

The result of all these innovations and thoughtful design touches is an outstanding studio monitor able to perform at a far higher level than the typical monitor—even in the most critical listening environment. Whether you’re mixing a dynamic 100-piece full orchestra, a full throttle rock band or even high-energy electronic music, your mixes will sound real, enabling you to make confident and precise adjustments to your recordings. That’s what a great monitor is supposed to do for you. Your mix is only as good as your monitor. Don’t settle for less than the best. Choose the M-Audio M3-8.

In-Line MF/HF Design

The midrange and tweeter are mounted in-line with each other (the tweeter is actually mounted at the apex of the midrange) at precisely the same point in space. Doing so means the sound of the two drivers integrates perfectly, without the “time/arrival distortion” of conventionally-spaced drive units. Instead, with the M-Audio M3-8, the all-important midrange and treble information reaches your ears at precisely the same time, delivering the ultimate in detail and clarity.

Kevlar Low and Mid-Frequency Drivers

Kevlar® is an ideal driver diaphragm material: Incredibly strong, stiff and light (it’s used in military body armor!). We use it in both the M3-8’s 8” woofer and the 5” midrange, giving those units an incredibly fast transient attack, excellent sensitivity and great resistance to cone flexing and resonant breakup at high power levels. Loud, strong and fast—perfect monitor characteristics.

Silk Dome Tweeter with Ferro-Fluid Cooling

The 1” woven silk dome tweeter uses Ferro-fluid voice coil cooling for added reliability at studio power levels and this tweeter imparts virtually no harshness or “listening fatigue,” as is often the case with metal dome tweeters. This is extremely important to the recording engineer who spends hours in the studio listening to their mixes, relying on their unfatigued ears to make subtle adjustments.

Custom Waveguide

The tweeter is mounted in an integrated waveguide, which precisely focuses and directs the treble energy into the listening field, ensuring a solid and stable sonic image and minimizing the random acoustic “scatter” that would distract from properly evaluating the recording’s playback.

Precision-Designed Baffle and Cabinet

The attractive baffle is chamfered at the top edges to aid in dispersion and reduce audibly-destructive edge diffraction. We’ve also optimized the cabinet’s internal bracing to ensure that the drivers have a solid, vibration-free platform from which to launch their sound, and we’ve used advanced computer software to determine and calibrate the port’s characteristics for the best possible bass response.

Three Individual Class AB Amplifiers

Three individual internal amplifiers provide power to each of the three speaker elements.  These amplifiers are specifically tailored to the drivers they are matched with, for smooth operation and system synergy that is virtually impossible to equal using outboard amplifiers and passive speakers. A total of 220 watts of Class AB amplification empowers the M3-8 with ample headroom for even the loudest mixes. The carefully-selected crossover points divide the frequency spectrum into very precise bands so that each individual driver only handles the exact frequency range where it is most accurate.
